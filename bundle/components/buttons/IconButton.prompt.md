Square, icon-only button for toolbars and compact controls. Icon must be a single-weight outline SVG (`stroke: currentColor`, `fill: none`) from the Zuper outline set.

```jsx
<IconButton ariaLabel="Search" variant="outline">
  <svg width="20" height="20" viewBox="0 0 24 24" fill="none" stroke="currentColor" stroke-width="1.7" stroke-linecap="round"><circle cx="11" cy="11" r="7"/><path d="m21 21-4.3-4.3"/></svg>
</IconButton>
```

Variants: `primary | outline | ghost`. Sizes `sm (32) | md (40) | lg (48)`.
