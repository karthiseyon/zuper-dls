# Social & display ads

## Two looks, one CTA each

1. **Orange gradient ground:** `linear-gradient(135deg,#1C0A06 0%,#7E1C0B 30%,#D43A14 64%,#F2864F 100%)` (dark top-left → light), white text, white logo lockup, **black** CTA button ("Book a demo").
2. **White card:** 1px `#E7E0D6` border, an 8-10px orange-gradient bar across the top, orange Geist Mono eyebrow, dark logo, **orange** CTA button.

Headlines: Plus Jakarta Sans 800, tight tracking (-.02 to -.04em), short and benefit-led ("Win 1 in 3 quotes on-site", "First call to invoice"). One CTA per unit. CTAs link to https://www.zuper.co/demo.

## Sizes

- **Social:** Instagram 1080×1080 · Story/Reel 1080×1920 · Link/OG 1200×630
- **Display (IAB):** leaderboard 728×90 · billboard 970×250 · medium rectangle 300×250 · large rectangle 336×280 · half page 300×600 · skyscraper 160×600 · mobile 320×100

Build each at its real pixel size in HTML, then export.

## Zuper Sense creatives

Sense-topic creatives use the dark look: `#09090B` ground, `#FF8B5A` orange-on-dark, zinc text (`#FAFAFA`/`#A1A1AA`). Same one-CTA rule.

## Show templates (e.g. "Raise the Roof" Facebook Live, 1280×720)

Orange gradient ground (dark top-left → light) · tilted white "RAISE THE ROOF" sticker badge top-left · Zuper logo + event lockup · orange-outline `FACEBOOK LIVE` pill · bold white headline · date/time row · speaker panel on the right. A full-bleed thin-line grid frames the layout with small filled 4-point sparkle marks at intersections. Speakers are text only (name, role, company - no speaker logos); photos fill each cell. One layout, four variants by speaker count (1/2/3/4); keep the chrome fixed, swap date and speakers per episode.
