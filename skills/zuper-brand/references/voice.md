# Zuper voice

How Zuper writes, from a homepage to a button label. The test for every line: if a roofing contractor wouldn't say it at a trade show, it doesn't belong.

## The five principles

1. **Plain-spoken.** Write like you're talking to a friend at a trade show. 8th-grade words, no hyperbole. Industry terms techs use daily are fine; vendor-speak isn't.
2. **Approachable.** Warm and direct. Contractions, short sentences, second person. Professional warmth, not startup-bro.
3. **Customer-obsessed.** Lead with the outcome, then the capability. The customer is the hero of every section; name the pain in their words, not the product's.
4. **Celebrate field teams.** Name the roles: field techs, crew leads, installers. Show their real day (a crew lead juggling six jobs across three zip codes). Respect them; never "manage resources."
5. **Forward-looking.** Confidently optimistic. Future-tense outcomes ("you'll see…"). No hedging, no hype.

## Hard rules

- Write to "you." Use "we" only to show how we help. If a paragraph opens with three "we"s, rewrite it around the reader.
- Real numbers over adjectives: "Win 1 in 3 quotes on-site" beats "dramatically improve win rates."
- Sentence case everywhere, including headlines. One claim per headline.
- **No em dashes.** Use commas, periods, colons or parentheses.
- Active voice. Say who does what.

## Banned vocabulary

seamless · leverage · unlock · empower · streamline · robust · cutting-edge · game-changing · elevate · harness · synergy · ecosystem · holistic · revolutionary · "in today's fast-paced world"

## Structure tells to avoid (they read as AI or salesy)

- Throat-clearing openers ("In the world of field service…"). Start with the reader's problem.
- Hollow wrap-ups ("In conclusion, Zuper is the right choice…"). End on the outcome or the CTA.
- Reflexive triads ("faster, smarter, better"). Pick the one that matters.
- "Not just X, Y" parallelism ("not just software, a partner"). Make the claim directly.

## On-tone vs off-tone

- Off: "We leverage cutting-edge AI to seamlessly streamline your operations."
- On: "Quote the job before you leave the driveway. Zuper prices it from your costs, so the margin's right every time."

- Off: "Our robust platform empowers organizations to unlock efficiency."
- On: "Your crew leads see every job, every tech, every day, on one board."
