From spreadsheets to scale

How a family-owned, #1-rated Missouri roofing company replaced a patchwork of CRM, spreadsheets and photo apps with one platform — and saw results in two months.

Dickinson Roofing, based in Sedalia, Missouri, built its reputation on quality workmanship and insurance-claim expertise. But like many growing operations, their tools couldn't keep pace with their ambitions — until they found Zuper.

The challenge

Scheduling was flying blind. The legacy dispatch board showed a job existed — but not its size, pitch, or status. The team kept a separate spreadsheet to fill the gap.

Upsells were left on the table. Reps mentally filtered out premium options before presenting them, defaulting to lower tiers.

Field documentation was scattered. Notes lived in the CRM, photos in a separate app — and neither talked to the other.

Finance ran on friction. Paper receipts sorted by hand, and a daily trip to the bank to deposit checks.

The shift: Zuper doesn't patch holes — it replaces the patchwork entirely with one system that runs the operation end to end.
